HOW TO INSTALL DECKBOARD IF THE ERROR "THE DEVICE CAN'T CONNECT" POP UP
Hello guys for the installation of deckboard you have to install the adb driver. Search on chrome adb driver (for your specific device)or click on the link --> "link_adb_driver_installer__"and install them. As a second step estract and open the folder, then connect with an usb cable your phone to the PC and go on device manager, left-click on portable device and then right-click on the name of your device. Now a drop down menu will appear, you have to left-click on update driver. Now click on search the driver software in the PC and browse to the usb_driver folder. Then click next and wait until the installation is finished. P.S. Before of all, check if you have developer settings enabled because USB DEBUG must be on.
